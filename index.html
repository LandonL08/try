Mental Illness

We are all in this together.



You're not Alone.

While many think that they're the only one going through their illness, they're actually wrong. According to the CDC (Centers for Disease Control), more than 1/5 American adults are in the same boat and have a mental illness.

Speak about it.

As I previously mentioned, many people who have a mental illness begin to feel very alone, hence why we must speak to others about it.

Whether it be speaking to a friend, family member, or a therapist, just talking to someone can "reduce feelings of anxiety, isolation, and loneliness." (Summa Health)

How to support those who live with a mental illness.

  • Be understanding.
  • Have an open mind.
  • Use appropriate language.
  • Ask questions.
  • Be there for them.
